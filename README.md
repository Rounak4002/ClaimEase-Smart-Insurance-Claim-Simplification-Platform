## Description
________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
### ClaimEase is a backend-driven insurance claim management platform designed to simplify the process of claim submission, tracking, and operational review. The system focuses on reducing complexity, improving transparency, and modeling real-world insurance claim workflows using clean REST APIs and structured backend logic.
### Insurance claim processing often involves multiple steps, document validations, and status updates, which can become confusing for customers and time-consuming for operations teams. ClaimEase addresses these challenges by providing a structured and simplified claim workflow that ensures clarity at every stage of the claim lifecycle.
### The platform enables users to submit insurance claims with essential details and track their claim status in real time. On the backend, claims are managed through RESTful services that handle claim intake, persistence, and status transitions. This mirrors how insurance organizations process claims internally while maintaining clean separation of responsibilities within the system.
### The project emphasizes backend engineering fundamentals such as API design, data modeling, and business workflow implementation. ClaimEase is intentionally designed to be modular and extensible, allowing future enhancements such as role-based access control, analytics dashboards, or microservice-based scaling.
### This project demonstrates the ability to understand and solve business problems using technology, aligning closely with modern insurance-tech platforms that aim to simplify customer experience and operational efficiency.
